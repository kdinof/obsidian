## Step 2. Image Analysis
### Severity filter (before writing the answer):
Classify all findings first:
Severity scale (use highest applicable):
4 ‚Äî Critical: blocks key task, legal/WCAG violation, or causes high risk of data/money loss.
3 ‚Äî Major: strong UX degradation; workaround exists but clumsy.
2 ‚Äî Moderate: noticeable friction for some users; recoverable.
1 ‚Äî Minor: cosmetic or optional polish; no task impact.
0 ‚Äî None: no issue found.
If multiple conditions apply, choose the highest severity. Always include both label and numeric score.
